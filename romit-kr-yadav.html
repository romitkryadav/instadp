About Romit Kr Yadav

Hello! My name is Romit Kr Yadav, and I am the creator of InstaDPdownload.

This site was created to help you see and download an Instagram user's profile picture in its full resolution. The first thing I found after creating my Instagram account was how annoying it is to only see a user’s profile picture in a tiny circular format. With my love for building online tools, I thought how great would it be to create a tool to solve this problem?

I like to build online tools, learn about SEO, build websites, and figure out how search engines work. Most of my time is spent testing out new ideas, improving my websites, and building online tools to help people with things that they use on a daily basis.

My overall goal with InstaDPdownload is to keep it fast, simple, and available to anyone without having to go through a bunch of complicated steps or downloads.

My Journey

My journey began with no team, no money, and no tech background; just curiosity and a singular goal – to create useful websites that people want to use.

I learned about SEO, tools, and digital businesses after completing my 12th board exams. Instead of waiting for opportunities to present themselves, I decided to create them. I spent a lot of time learning how websites work and how to use search engines and analyze competitors and test different ideas for tools.

What I realised is that each day millions of people are searching for simple online tools so I started to create downloader sites, profile viewer sites, counters and other utility type sites. I wasn't looking to develop complex products. I was looking to develop tools that would quickly and easily resolve one single issue.

Eventually, I released several projects, including InstaDPdownload and ThreadsGrab. Through each project, I also gained insight into how to optimize a website for SEO, create content, understand user experience, optimize a website for performance and increase the amount of traffic being driven to a website by search engines. All of my websites taught me something new, whether it was ranking web pages, fixing technical issues, improving content quality, or figuring out exactly what users really need.

My experiences as an independent creator mirror those of many others. I have experimented with many things—from successful projects to failures. These each provided me with additional knowledge regarding how to build an online business.

At this time, I am continuing to build practical and easy-to-use web-based tools. My overall objective is simple: Create things people will want to use while still improving my techniques in search engine optimization (SEO), web development, and entrepreneurial development.

I am still learning, still creating, and continuing toward larger objectives. This is only the start of my journey.
