The Importance of Following in the Future of Marketing

A lot of social media experts believe that engagement is more important than having followers, which is partially true. But, in the long-run, followers are still one of the best ways to measure growth and visibility for both brands and audiences.

When accounts consistently gain followers, that’s usually a sign that their content is resonating with more people. Growth is often indicative of an effective strategy for creating content, retaining an audience, and obtaining more visibility via Instagram's recommendation engine.

To businesses, an increasing number of followers means more awareness and preference for their brand. To creators, the number of followers indicates whether their audience is enjoying their content. To marketers, follower trends will provide insight into how well their campaigns are performing and what they can do to improve their efforts.

A Helpful Tool for Creators and Businesses

For creators wanting to grow their audience, it may seem like a long struggle. There are times when the number of followers you have changes so little that you don't see any increase in your number of followers, and then there are also times when your number of followers may increase dramatically overnight.

Having access to a follower counter allows you to track and monitor the changes to your number of followers without having to refresh Instagram every few moments.

Businesses also have different uses for a follower tracking system as compared to creators. They may want to see if a marketing campaign is working to drive traffic toward an individual product, or they may just want to know if they have attracted more followers after launching a new product, and using a follower tracking solution will help them to know if they are gaining more followers after a marketing campaign.

Social media managers and agencies can also utilize a follower tracking service to measure how well each of their clients' accounts are performing. Rather than having to check each account individually, they can check all the various accounts at the same time so they can see how each of them is performing relative to all of the other accounts.
