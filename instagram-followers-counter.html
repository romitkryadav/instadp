Instagram Growth Isn't Always Linear

Many people have made the mistake of believing their follower count will grow steadily day-to-day.

In reality, Instagram's growth is rarely straight and consistent in the number of followers you gain on any given day. In some instances, you gain followers and in others you lose them. Every type of account, even the biggest creators, experiences this.

What matters is the direction and trend of the growth. If you're increasing your number of followers progressively month after month, you're on the right track. Daily gradual increases and decreases are common and shouldn't cause you distress.

How Does Your Instagram Account Grow so Much All of a Sudden?

There are many possible reasons you can experience a recent spike in your follower count on Instagram.

One of the most common reasons for gaining followers is an increasingly popular Reel. The way Instagram's algorithm works allows users who don't yet follow you to see your Reels often. If someone likes what they see, they'll likely want to follow your account for more content.

Another major reason for followers coming to you quickly is through collaboration. When two people collaborate on a project, both audiences get to see new content and personalities. This means that you can both gain new followers.

There are so many things that can create sudden spikes in your follower counts - trending topics and viral moments, giveaways, news stories, a larger user's share, and much more.

Because of the many ways to gain followers, you should pay close attention to how each of your posts performs in terms of gaining followers.
